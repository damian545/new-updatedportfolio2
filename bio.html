ABOUT ME AND WHAT DRIVES ME.

Hello, my name is Damian Tapp. I'm 46 years old and I'm studying to be a web designer. I have had no coding experience until now, I have recently completed an intense 6 month coding bootcamp. I have always been a very creative person and I am always looking at ways to better the things I have made because I am fussy. Whether it was with building my drift car or fixing a down pipe on my house, I wont shy away from a challenge. I have done a lot of things in my life for the first time and I will probably do a lot more. So building websites, is no different to building my retaining wall to me. It might be time consuming and confusing at times but very rewarding with the finished product, which is the most exciting part of any project. The more creative I get, the more I learn about exciting techniques to enhance websites and this is what I love about web designing.
